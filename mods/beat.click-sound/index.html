Click Sounds

Plays a click sound whenever you press a jump button, or (optionally) a release sound whenever you release a jump button. If you want a new built-in click sound or feature to be added, join the discord server. <3

Uses Geode v2.0.0-beta.20 and Node-IDs v1.6.1

Open the mod settings to change the click sound. Set the click sound to '0' to use a custom click sound.
